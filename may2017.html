  • Aisha Pew (Dovecote) and B.Cole (Brioxy)
  • Derick Ebert, Baltimore's Youth Poet Laureate
  • Amorous Ebony, powerhouse vocalist
  • Clarence Ward III, saxophonist & trumpeter

Bring your vibrant energy, an open mind, and a friend to this FREE event!
