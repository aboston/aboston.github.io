SunSets is a tribute to the cultural salons that nurtured the celebrated artists and thinkers of the Harlem Renaissance. This monthly gathering will connect Baltimore’s creative community for a relaxed evening of artistic exchange, expression, and collaboration.

Join us on Friday, October 16th from 7-10pm at NuBohemia Café (42 W. Biddle Street, Baltimore, MD) for our second SunSets session.

Come kick it, sip some wine, and learn more about the vision and intention behind this emerging space. Bring your projects, an open mind, and a friend!
