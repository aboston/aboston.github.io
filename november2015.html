• SunSets

November 2015


SunSets is a tribute to the cultural salons that nurtured the celebrated artists and thinkers of the Harlem Renaissance. This monthly gathering will connect Baltimore’s creative community for a chill evening of artistic exchange, expression, and collaboration.

Come hang out on FRIDAY, November 20th from 7-10PM at NuBohemia (42 W. Biddle Street, Baltimore, MD) for our third SunSets session where we will explore the theme of STORYTELLING.

Featured presenters include:

  • Heather Smith
  • Hope Marsh
  • Jason Harris
  • Bilphena Yahwon
  • Amadou Kouyate

As always, bring your projects, an open mind, and a friend! Free entry. Free wine.
